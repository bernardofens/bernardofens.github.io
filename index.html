01 — About

I'm a Computer Science student at PUCRS and iOS developer at the Apple Developer Academy, where I've been building real products inside the Apple ecosystem for over two years.

My work spans the full Apple platform — iPhone, iPad, macOS, watchOS and visionOS — using Swift, SwiftUI, UIKit and SpriteKit. I care about design, craft and shipping.

I work in multidisciplinary teams with SCRUM methodology, handle UX/UI design in Figma, do QA testing via AppStore Connect and regularly pitch products to audiences ranging from developers to investors.

Beyond code, I'm a creative — the art, music and code in Shiro. are all mine. I like to understand the full product, not just my slice of it.
