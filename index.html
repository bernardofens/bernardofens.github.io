Health · iOS 2025

Clairfy

Medical consultations are full of jargon. Patients leave confused and lose important information. Clairfy solves this by generating instant, clear summaries for both doctors and patients — from audio.

Problem
Doctors speak in medical language. Patients forget 40–80% of what they're told. No existing app focused on the consultation itself — only on post-visit records.
Process
CBL + SCRUM — challenge framing, user interviews, rapid prototyping in Figma, iterative Swift development across multiple sprints. Evaluated at every cycle by the Academy.
My role
iOS development (Swift/SwiftUI), UX design, QA via TestFlight, App Store submission, and pitching — including the Startup Garage Top 10 final round and the BioHub/Tecnopuc podcast.
Result
Live on the App Store with international distribution. Top 10 at Startup Garage. Featured on BioHub podcast.
Swift SwiftUI AVFoundation App Store Connect TestFlight Figma SCRUM

Creative · iOS 2025

Ziner

Creating zines requires complex desktop tools or a lot of manual work. Ziner makes it possible on iPhone — full 8-page editor, PDF export ready for printing, right in your pocket.

Problem
No dedicated mobile app exists for the zine format. The format has strict constraints (8-page folding) that no general design tool respects.
Process
CBL methodology — researched zine communities, mapped pain points, prototyped folding logic, designed a focused UX in Figma, implemented PDF generation with PDFKit and SwiftUI canvas.
My role
Swift/SwiftUI development, PDFKit integration, UX design following Apple HIG, QA and App Store release.
Result
Live on the App Store with international distribution. The only dedicated zine editor on iOS.
Swift SwiftUI PDFKit UX Design Figma App Store Connect
App Store ↗
Game · iOS 2025

Shiro.

A solo creative challenge: build a complete game — code, pixel art, and original music — from scratch. Result: a vertical climbing game with a retro Game Boy aesthetic and Game Center leaderboards.

Challenge
Build a complete game experience solo — including all creative assets — that feels cohesive and is fun to play. Prove end-to-end product ownership.
Process
Designed the game loop, created all pixel art assets, composed original chiptune music (piano background → game composition), implemented physics in SpriteKit, integrated Game Center. Every decision was intentional — visual coherence, audio feedback, progression feel.
My role
100% solo — game design, Swift/SpriteKit development, all pixel art, original music composition, QA, App Store submission.
Result
Live on App Store. Global and local Game Center leaderboards. A fully original creative product — code, art and music — by one person.
Swift SpriteKit Game Center Pixel Art Original Music Solo Project
